About Us

Welcome to Pushpendra Computer Typing Institute, your trusted destination for professional typing education in Jarud. We specialize in Government Commercial Certificate (GCC) and Government Computer Typing Certificate (GCC-TBC) courses approved by Maharashtra State Examination Council (MSCE), Pune.


Our institute offers comprehensive training in English, Marathi, and Hindi typing with speeds ranging from 30 to 60 words per minute. With experienced instructors and modern infrastructure, we ensure that every student achieves certification and professional competency.


Located conveniently near the bus stop in Jarud, we provide a conducive learning environment with the latest technology and personalized attention to help you succeed in your typing career.
